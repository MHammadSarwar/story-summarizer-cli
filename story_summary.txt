A sparrow, desperate for food during a dry summer, found a large sunflower with ripe seeds. 
However, the seeds were locked inside the flower's tough center, beyond her reach.  
Seeking help, the sparrow saw a family of ants working near the sunflower's roots.  
She offered them a plan: she would shake the stalk to release the seeds, and they would provide the strength.
The ants agreed, and started gnawing on the base of the stem. 
The sparrow used her wings to make the sunflower bend, while the ants worked tirelessly to loosen the seeds.   
All of their efforts created a shower of seeds, shared between the sparrow and the ants.  
This experience highlights the power of teamwork and mutual cooperation in achieving successful outcomes. 
Even in seemingly overwhelming challenges, working together can help overcome obstacles and ensure survival.
